CS202 - Assignment 1


I used to be part of Civil Air Patrol. I was volunteering as a crossing guard for the Arlington Fly-In 2019 event which is essentially an airshow that takes place at Arlington Municipal Airport (AWO).
I was pulled aside to join on an Orientation Ride (O-Ride).
I was seated with a senior pilot and I was placed in the co-pilot seat in a Cessna 182 which is a powered single engine aircraft.
The Civil Air Patrol teaches situational awareness which meant I had to keep an active eye on environment, weather conditions and the instrument panel.
At some point, we flew about 3 minutes over the Canadian-American border before making our way back to AWO.
My senior pilot and I were then told that a Canadian Air Traffic Control Tower attempted to hail us, but we had turned around by then. Luckily, they assumed we were from the Arlington event.
